EcoFlow DELTA Portable Power Station Review

The EcoFlow DELTA portable power station is one of the most powerful solar generators available for home backup, camping and outdoor use. With a large battery capacity and fast charging technology, it can power many devices during power outages or outdoor adventures.

Main Features

  • 1260Wh lithium battery capacity
  • 1800W AC output (3300W surge)
  • Fast recharge from 0-80% in about 1 hour
  • Multiple outputs including AC outlets, USB-C and USB-A
  • Compatible with portable solar panels

What Can It Power?

Thanks to its powerful inverter, the EcoFlow DELTA can run devices such as laptops, routers, televisions, mini refrigerators, lighting systems and even some power tools. This makes it a popular solution for emergency backup power and off-grid living.

Pros

  • Very powerful for a portable power station
  • Extremely fast charging technology
  • Large battery capacity
  • Reliable brand in portable energy solutions

Cons

  • Heavier than smaller portable generators
  • Solar panels sold separately
